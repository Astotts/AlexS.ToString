Decay & Devotion

Prelude: The Indev Expo


-Decay & Devotion (The original pitch)

-Game: Multiplayer Co-Op and optionally single player story mode you and your pregnant wife hit the road in an attempt to get away from the coming zombie horde, you stop at crossroads to refuel your vehicle, or clear blockages you traverse the continental united states in an effort to find safe haven. You'll be defending your vehicle and thus wife from zombie hordes with a group of survivors you've met along the way. You'll collect resources from the world and traders during the day to build defenses for the night phase.

Getting the Gang Together. .


As president of the game development club I was in charge of hosting the In development exhibition (Indev Expo) where I would successfully network with a developer in the industry looking to move into game development. With this contact I created a team along side two other students eventually inviting on a third.

Decay & Devotion is currently in development slated for release in late August
